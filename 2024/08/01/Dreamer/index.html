DREAM TO CONTROL: LEARNING BEHAVIORS BY LATENT IMAGINATION

Dreamer: Algorithm

background and motivations

  1. world models from high-dimensional sensory inputs can be learned through DL

highlights and contributions

  1. learns long-horizon behaviors purely by latent imagination
  2. optimize actor by propagating gradients of multi-step values back through latent dynamics

World Model Learning

Latent imagination requires a representation model, transition model and reward model

reward prediction

Simply learning to predict future rewards given actions and past observation

reconstruction (PlaNet, 2018)

Learns world model by reconstructing observations via variational information bottleneck (VIB) objective
