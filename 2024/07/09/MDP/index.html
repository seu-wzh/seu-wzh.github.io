  4. 奖励函数 R:S×A↦R=E(Rt+1∣St=s, At=a)\mathcal{R} : \mathcal{S} \times \mathcal{A} \mapsto \mathbb{R} = \mathcal{E}(R_{t + 1} \mid S_{t} = s,\ A_{t} = a)R:S×A↦R=E(Rt+1​∣St​=s, At​=a)
