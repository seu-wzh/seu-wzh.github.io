Model Predictive Path Integral（MPPI）

Parameter Initialization

To reduce the number of iterations required for convergence, TD-MPC reuses the 1-step shifted mean value μτ\mu_{\tau}μτ​ obtained at the previous step, but always use a large initial variance to avoid local minima.

Exploration by Planning

To promote consistent exploration, TD-MPC constrains the std. deviation by updating as
