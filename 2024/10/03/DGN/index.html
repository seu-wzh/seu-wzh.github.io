General Structure

DGN views the multi-agent environment as a dynamic coordination graph varing over time, where the adjacency among agents is determined by a specific metric like distance and the neighbouring agents can communicate with each other

The agent iii fuses the local information with its neighbours N(i)N(i)N(i) through multi-head attention in a convolutional layer

The network parameters θ\thetaθ are shared by all agents and trained through Target Q-Learning algorithm end-to-end
