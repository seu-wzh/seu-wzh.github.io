The semantic similarity between goal gkg_{k}gk​ and language description embedding of transition utu_{t}ut​ is

w(ut∣gk)=ut⋅gk∥ut∥∥gk∥⋅I[ut⋅gk∥ut∥∥gk∥>M]w(u_{t} \mid g_{k}) = \frac{u_{t} \cdot g_{k}}{\| u_{t} \| \| g_{k} \|} \cdot \mathbb{I} \left[ \frac{u_{t} \cdot g_{k}}{\| u_{t} \| \| g_{k} \|} > M \right] w(ut​∣gk​)=∥ut​∥∥gk​∥ut​⋅gk​​⋅I[∥ut​∥∥gk​∥ut​⋅gk​​>M]
